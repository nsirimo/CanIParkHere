Welcome to CanIParkHere!

Hello there, if you are using this app, then you are finally tired of the Los Angeles County parking. Mainly, you are interested in wanting to know where exactly can you park at the destination you chose. Well, you are in good hands. Welcome to the CanIParkHere App. We are here to help you find a parking within the Los Angeles County.

Who are we?

We are a team of students at California State University, Los Angeles majoring in Computer Science. Besides as students, we also face the struggle of finding parking and trying to figure out if we are allowed to park there without getting a ticket. Lets face it, parking is a drag. Mainly, if you are trying to park in Los Angeles.
